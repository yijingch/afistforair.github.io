---
title: Patient pair
date: 2021-07-06 20:23:00 +08:00
tags: [poem]
---


They shared a cell for a while.  
Made small talks.   
Heard halted sighs and replied with another.  
Swallowed the same pack of pills.  
Checked-in weekly.     
Played with relapses.    
They simply   
did   
not   
know   
how to deal with each other.
